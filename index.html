About Us

We are a group of young and enthusiastic artists who love to share the knowledge we have with the world! Below you will find more information about each tutor and when the next classes are scheduled for. First come, first served so dont hesitate to Sign Up for your next adventure with us!

Christian

Christian is an experience painter who works mostly with oil and acrylic. Large oil paintings on canvas gets his blood flowing.

Rosa

Rosa is a world-renowned sculptor. She prefers to work with Marble and clay but can master most materials.

Daniel

Daniel is our in-house computer wizard. He loves making digital art and NFTs in all shapes and forms is his current obsession.
